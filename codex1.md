Design a cute, kawaii-style gacha machine interactive UI for a "bucin" (lovesick/devoted) theme.

Visual concept:
- A classic round capsule toy vending machine (gachapon style), rendered in soft pastel colors — think blush pink, lavender, mint, and creamy white
- The machine body should be chunky and rounded, like a real Japanese gachapon machine with a transparent dome on top filled with colorful capsule balls
- Warm glowing light effect inside the dome
- Decorative elements: small hearts, stars, sparkles, tiny flowers scattered around the machine
- A coin slot and a big, satisfying round crank/button labeled something cute like "💖 Putar!" or "✨ Minta Pesan"

Interaction flow (design as 3 screens/frames):

Frame 1 — Idle State:
- The gacha machine sits centered on screen, looking adorable and inviting
- Button/crank is glowing softly, pulsing gently
- Small floating hearts around the machine

Frame 2 — Dispensing Animation:
- A capsule ball (opaque, colorful — pink or red) rolls out of the slot at the bottom
- Motion blur / bouncing effect on the capsule
- Confetti and sparkles burst around the machine
- The dome shows fewer balls inside (one was taken)

Frame 3 — Opening the Capsule:
- The capsule ball is centered on screen, larger
- It cracks open like an egg/shell with a satisfying split animation
- Inside: a small folded paper or love letter unfolds
- The message reveals on a soft parchment-style card, in handwritten font
- Example message: "Kamu terlalu manis untuk diabaikan 🍬" or "Hari ini pun, aku mikirin kamu 💌"
- A "Putar Lagi?" button appears at the bottom

Style notes:
- Font: Use a cute handwritten or rounded font (like Nunito, Pacifico, or Quicksand)
- Color palette: blush pink #FFB5C8, soft lavender #D4BAFF, mint #B5EAD7, cream #FFF9F0
- Shadows: soft drop shadows, no harsh lines
- Overall vibe: sweet, cozy, like a gift from someone who loves you
- Make it feel like a physical toy — chunky, tactile, satisfying to interact with

Deliver as a complete interactive prototype with component variants for each state.
